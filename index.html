Moringa Bank

Moringa Bank (Uganda), commonly known as Moringa Bank,
is a commercial bank in Kenya. It is one of the commercial banks
licensed by the CBK , the central bank and national banking
regulator. EBU is a subsidiary of Exim Bank Tanzania, which also has
subsidiaries in Tanzania, Comoros, and Djibouti.
